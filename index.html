New study options

ReMSTEP is developing new study options at each partner intitution to produce graduate teachers better equipped in maths and science teaching, and improve the visibility of teaching as a career for high calibre maths and science students.

Learn more

Additional experiences

ReMSTEP is working with specialist maths and science centres to develop and deliver additional experiences for pre-service teachers that give them access to practising mathemeticians and scientists.

Learn more

Exemplars

Products and process models created through the additional experience activities will be made available as exemplar resources for teacher education providers, pre-service and in-service teachers.
